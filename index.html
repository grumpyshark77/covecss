I gravitate toward accessibility and education because both require you to think about people who aren't you. That's a good habit for any developer to build

EmpathyLens

EmpathyLens lets you experience your own website through the eyes of someone with a visual, cognitive, or motor impairment. Colour blindness filters, blur, dyslexia simulation, hand tremor, and a live WCAG contrast checker all run directly in your browser without touching a single line of your code.
